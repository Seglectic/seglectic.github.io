Today was basically an all-day UI session. 

## Gallery
- Thumbnails got the occlusion percentage in the top-left corner with a drop shadow and custom font.
- Status mark moved to the bottom-right corner instead.
- other smoll stuff, matters for readability at a glance though. 

## Map
- Controls got simplified
- Drawer layout got refined
- Added a collapsible control tray so the map view doesn't feel cluttered 
- Connection indicator was ending up below the buttons for some reason and it was kind of a pain to fix
- The map bubble count icon was getting covered by the pins it was supposed to be labeling, also kind of a pain
- Labels and badges overall got another pass.

## Live Sync
- Live sync now includes deletes, so if something gets removed on the backend it actually disappears from the client without a refresh.

Lots of polish commits today, Just tons of minor tweaking but the app feels noticeably more coherent now.

The theme's better, and it's starting to feel field-ready.
